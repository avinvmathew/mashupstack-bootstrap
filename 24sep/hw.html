    FAQs

    A high score is the highest number of points a player has earned in a game. To get one, focus on maximizing points by learning combo attacks, destroying difficult enemies, and collecting all bonus items. Avoid getting hit, as many games deduct points for taking damage. Some games also reward you with more points for beating a level quickly or for using a specific weapon.

    Special attacks, or "super moves," are powerful abilities that can clear the screen of enemies or deal massive damage to a boss. They are usually tied to a special meter that fills up as you play. This meter charges as you defeat enemies, collect power-ups, or perform successful combos. Once the meter is full, you can press a specific button or a combination of buttons to unleash the attack. Be careful, as these attacks often have a cost, and you might lose your meter or some health after using it.

    Many older arcade games have cheat codes that can give you extra lives, unlock special characters, or start you at a later level. These codes are often a sequence of button presses (e.g., Up, Up, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right, B, A). However, some modern arcade games, especially those focused on online leaderboards, might not have cheat codes. A great alternative is to look for "tricks" or "glitches" online, as these can often give you an advantage without using a traditional code.
